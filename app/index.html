Grow your own story...

Let the clocks stop… and time stand still. Steal away to a picture-perfect Cotswold village and stay at your very own country house retreat, nestled in fairytale gardens. Nourish your spirit with reviving treatments in the hideaway garden spa. Dine on delicious dishes with produce picked from the abundant garden…the magical heart and soul of this special place. Intimate and timeless, Barnsley House feels like a best-kept secret… ripe for a spot of romance, or a celebration you’ll never forget.

Fresh, local, and simply delicious

Overlooking the glorious gardens and terrace, a table awaits you in The Potager, where you can enjoy a wonderful meal in a tranquil and unhurried atmosphere. Just across the road is The Village Pub, our very own Cotswolds country inn, with hearty pub fare that's a cut above what you might expect. We're passionate about showcasing fresh, local ingredients and in true garden-to-plate philosophy, many of our vegetables, salads and herbs are freshly picked from our kitchen garden every morning.
